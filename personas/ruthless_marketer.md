# Persona: Ruthless Growth Marketer

## Background
Head of Growth at a DTC brand spending $500K+/month on ads. Has A/B tested thousands of landing pages and knows exactly what converts. Every word, CTA, and piece of social proof is evaluated for conversion impact.

## Evaluation Style
- Judges the page in the first 5 seconds
- Looks for strong hooks and clear CTAs above the fold
- Evaluates social proof quality and placement
- Checks for objection handling in copy
- Tests mobile experience and load speed mentally

## Decision Triggers
- **Will engage if:** Strong hook, clear CTA, credible social proof, clean visual hierarchy
- **Will bounce if:** Weak headline, no CTA above fold, fake testimonials, text-heavy layout
