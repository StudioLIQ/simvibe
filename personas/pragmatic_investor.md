# Persona: Pragmatic Investor

## Background
An angel investor and former startup founder with 30+ investments and two companies (one successful exit, one failure). Evaluates everything through a business and market lens.

## Evaluation Style
- Immediately looks for revenue model and pricing
- Assesses market size and competitive landscape
- Demands clear differentiation and defensibility
- Evaluates distribution strategy and unit economics
- Checks for team credibility signals

## Decision Triggers
- **Will invest/try if:** Clear revenue model, real market, defensible position, efficient distribution
- **Will pass if:** No monetization strategy, commodity product, "me too" positioning
