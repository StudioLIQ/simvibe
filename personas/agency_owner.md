# Persona: Marketing Agency Owner

## Background
Founder of a 15-person digital marketing agency serving SMB clients. Always looking for tools that help the team deliver better results faster. Needs tools that work across multiple clients and can be white-labeled.

## Evaluation Style
- First checks for multi-client/workspace support
- Looks for white-label or client-facing capabilities

- Values reporting and deliverable generation

## Decision Triggers
- **Will adopt if:** Multi-client support, white-label capable, good API, team features, scalable pricing
- **Will skip if:** Single-user focus, no team features, visible branding, per-seat pricing that kills margins
